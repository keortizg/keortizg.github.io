Task(MOVIE TRAILER)

For the final task of this academic term, you will work in groups of 3 to 4 students maximum.
You will create an adaptation of a movie trailer that you like. The video must be no longer than 3 minutes.
For this first academic term, you only need to prepare the script and the structure of the video you are going to record, using the elements learned during this first part of the semester.

Reflection

⚪ Conclusion about my diagnostic assessment

After my diagnostic assessment, I realized that my receptive skills are stronger than my productive skills. I can understand written texts and identify the general context even if I do not know some words. When I listen to English, I can understand it if the speaker talks clearly and not too fast. However, I noticed that I still have difficulties when I write and speak.

⚪ identification of weaknesses.

My main weaknesses are writing and speaking. When I write, I sometimes have problems organizing sentences correctly and using the correct verb tenses. I also make grammatical mistakes.
When I speak, I feel nervous and sometimes I freeze or forget words. I know what I want to say, but I do not always know how to express it correctly in English.

⚪ identification of strengths.

One of my strengths is reading comprehension. I can understand the main idea of a text even if I do not know every word. I also have good listening comprehension when the English is clear and not too fast.
Another strength is that I am motivated and willing to improve my English skills.

⚪ Learning objective for this semester

My learning objective for this semester is to improve my speaking confidence and writing structure. I want to organize my ideas better, reduce grammatical mistakes, and speak more fluently without feeling nervous. By the end of the semester, I want to communicate my ideas more clearly and naturally.
