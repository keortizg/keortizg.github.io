My Strategies

Speaking

Imitating

Find a model from a video or audio and try to imitate it — words, stress, pauses, and intonation. You can also sing along. Then compare the model with what you said and note the differences.
📎 Evidence required Recordings or videos of your imitation, and a written reflection comparing your version to the model.

Speaking

Self-recording

Choose a topic appropriate for your level and talk about it for a few minutes. Record yourself as naturally as possible. Then listen back and identify weak areas to improve.

Writing

Literature for Grammar

Read a short text (poem, essay, or story appropriate for your level). Analyze its grammar structures, imitate the style, and create your own original writing based on what you read.
📎 Evidence required Your original writing imitating the text's style, and a reflection about the grammar structures you practiced.

Dossier

Task(MOVIE TRAILER)

For the final task of this academic term, you will work in groups of 3 to 4 students maximum.
You will create an adaptation of a movie trailer that you like. The video must be no longer than 3 minutes.
For this first academic term, you only need to prepare the script and the structure of the video you are going to record, using the elements learned during this first part of the semester.
